Overview

Cat Jazz is an exploration of experimental animation. Unlike traditional animation, the emphasis is not on figurative characters but on audio-visual synchronization in experimental animation.

Inspiration

The inspiration for Cat Jazz came from my love for jazz music. When listening to jazz, I always think of cats, a kind of elegant and mysterious creature. Upon learning about experimental animation, I think it is a perfect medium to showcase my understanding of cats due to the following reasons:

  • Experimental animation is abstract, so it is easy to stimulate the imagination of the audience.
  • The human brain tends to automatically synchronize audio and visual information, so appropriate audio in experimental animation can assist in rendering the desired atmosphere while leaving enough room for the audience's imagination.

Therefore, I decided to create my own jazz song about cats and turn it into an experimental animation.
