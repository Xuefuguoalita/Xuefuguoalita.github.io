Bio

Fuguo Xue is a multimedia artist and UX designer based in New York, who is pursuing her master’s degree in Integrated Design & Media at New York University. Before that, she graduated from Nanyang Technological University Singapore in June 2023 with a Bachelor of Engineering Degree in Information Engineering & Media (Highest Distinction).

Fuguo’s passion for digital art began with her enrollment in the Issues in Animation Practice course taught by Vibeke Sorensen. Her experimental animation created in this course, Cat Jazz, was presented at the NTU ADM End of Semester Show 2020.

Since then, Fuguo has explored various art and design forms, including experimental animation, interactive experience design, PC games, interactive storytelling, and UI/UX design. Notably, the PC game she co-developed with her friends, Earthlings was presented at The International Conference of Undergraduate Research 2022 (ICUR 2022).

In 2023, Fuguo designed and developed the interactive installation Cometale, which was featured at Ars Electronica Festival 2023 in Linz, Austria.

Fuguo's academic interests revolve around emerging media, with a focus on interactive installations and AI-driven entertainment technology. With her solid engineering background, Fuguo hopes to use advanced technology to push the boundaries of creative practice.
