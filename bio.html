Bio

Fuguo Xue is a final year engineering undergraduate in Information Engineering and Media program at Nanyang Technological University (NTU). Her academic interest lies in the field of entertainment technology including digital art, music visualization, interactive installations, and PC game development, which is an interdisciplinary field combining engineering and art.

Fuguo’s interest in entertainment technology began during the spring semester of 2020 when she attended the course Issues in Animation Practice, taught by Prof. Vibeke Sorensen. She learned about the basic principles of experimental animation in the course and started to think about the relationship between art and the audience. Her final project Cat Jazz for this course was later showcased at NTU ADM End of Semester Show 2020.

Upon realizing her interest in digital art, Fuguo later attended several courses in digital art, visual communication, and interactive design. Her projects include experimental animation, PC games, interactive installation, UI design, and interactive fiction.

After learning and exploring for the past 3 years, Fuguo now aspires to pursue a career in entertainment technology upon graduation.
