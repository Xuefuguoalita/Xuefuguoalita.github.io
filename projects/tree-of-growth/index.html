Tree of Growth

2024

TIME FRAME: 1 month

MEDIA: interactive projection built with Max MSP, and a block made with woodboard and acrylic paint.

Inspired by L-systems, known for their ability to generate customized recursive patterns, Tree of Growth is an interactive projection that brings the art of tree planting to life. By simply placing a red box (the “seed”) on the floor at different positions, participants can “grow” a diverse array of trees, each with unique shapes and characteristics.
This project is a collaboration between Fuguo Xue and Jinyuan Liu.
