The Surface City

2024

TIME FRAME: 2 weeks

MEDIA: generative landscape done by p5.js coding and Adobe Illustrator.

The Surface City is a concept design and storytelling experience enhanced by creative coding. It aims to explore the patterns of cities with code and the potential of 2D tiles in creating 3D visual effects.
The Surface City is a collaboration between Fuguo and Chenyi. It is part of the Visible Cities: Workshop Exhibition organized by CritLab Review.
